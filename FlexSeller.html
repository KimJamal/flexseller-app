Pricing Calculator

The Pricing Calculator helps you determine the optimal selling price for your products based on costs, desired profit margin, and various adjustments.

How it works:

  • Quantity: Number of units you're selling (defaults to 1)
  • Buying Price: Cost per unit from your supplier. Can be entered in KSh or USD - click the currency badge to toggle
  • Supplier Discount: Choose between percentage (%) or fixed amount (KSh) discount
  • VAT Handling: Select whether your buying price includes VAT (Inclusive) or excludes it (Exclusive). Default rate is 16%
  • Additional Costs: Transport, Shipping, and Other costs are divided by quantity to get per-unit costs
  • Target Margin: Your desired profit margin percentage

Results shown:

  • Unit Cost: Total cost per unit after all calculations
  • Profit: Total profit for all units
  • Selling Price: Recommended price per unit and total for all units
Tip: Click the "Details" button to see a step-by-step breakdown of how the price was calculated.
Reverse Finder

The Reverse Finder helps you work backwards from a target selling price to determine the correct list price before discount.

When to use:

  • When you know what price you want to sell at after giving a discount
  • To determine the original listed price that will result in your target price after a percentage discount

How it works:

  • Target Price: The price you want customers to actually pay
  • Discount %: The percentage discount you plan to offer

The calculator will show you the List Price to set and the Discount Amount that will be deducted.

Formula: Listed Price = Target Price ÷ (1 - Discount%)
Totals Calculator

The Totals Calculator computes VAT-inclusive totals for quick order calculations.

How it works:

  • Quantity: Number of items
  • Unit Price: Price per item (before VAT)
  • VAT Rate: Percentage to apply (default 16%)

Results shown:

  • Subtotal: Quantity × Unit Price
  • VAT Amount: Subtotal × VAT Rate
  • Grand Total: Subtotal + VAT Amount
Use case: Perfect for quick invoicing or pricing checks when you need to add VAT to a simple order.
Currency & Exchange Rates

FlexSeller supports both Kenyan Shillings (KSh) and US Dollars (USD) with automatic conversion.

Features:

  • Toggle Currency: Click the "KSh" or "$" badge next to Buying Price or Shipping to switch currencies
  • Automatic Conversion: Values are instantly converted when you switch currencies
  • Custom Exchange Rate: Click the edit icon (pencil) next to shipping/buying price labels to set your own rate
  • Live Rates: Fetch current USD to KES exchange rates from the internet

Status Bar:

The bottom status bar always shows the current exchange rate, VAT rate, and whether VAT is set to Inclusive or Exclusive mode.

Note: Your custom exchange rate is saved and persists between sessions.
Product Information Search (PIS)

PIS lets you search electronic components by part number and view product details like description, specs, images, and datasheets.

How to use:

  • Open: Click the PIS tab in the ribbon or use Ctrl+4
  • Search: Type a part number (e.g. LM358, ESP32-WROOM-32) and press Enter or click Search
  • History: Recent searches appear as chips you can click to re-run instantly
  • Media: Click the product image to view it larger and use next/prev to browse media
Note: Search works automatically. No setup is required.
Built-in Calculator

A full-featured calculator is available for quick calculations without leaving the app.

How to use:

  • Open: Click "View" in the ribbon, then "Calculator" or use F9
  • Basic Operations: +, −, ×, ÷, %
  • Clear: AC (All Clear), CE (Clear Entry), ← (Backspace)
  • History: Shows your previous calculation above the display

Keyboard Support:

When the calculator is open, use number keys, operators (+, −, *, /), Enter (=), Escape (AC), and Backspace.
